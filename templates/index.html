Case Studies

Examples of Enterprise Delivery

FinTech · Transaction Risk Platform

Problem: High false positives in fraud checks were slowing down payments.

Solution: Built a rules and ML-driven engine to score transactions in real time.

Technologies: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, AWS.

Result: 40% reduction in false positives and sub-second decision latency.

SaaS · Multi-Tenant Analytics Platform

Problem: Legacy architecture could not support new tenants without downtime.

Solution: Designed a multi-tenant architecture with isolated data domains.

Technologies: React, Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, Kafka, Kubernetes.

Result: 5× tenant capacity with zero-downtime onboarding.

Healthcare · Patient Engagement Portal

Problem: Fragmented patient systems created friction for patients and staff.

Solution: Implemented a unified portal with secure messaging and scheduling.

Technologies: Vue, Node.js, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, cloud-native hosting.

Result: 30% increase in digital self-service adoption.

E-Commerce · Order Management Platform

Problem: Spikes in order volume were causing stock inaccuracies and delayed fulfillment.

Solution: Built a centralized order and inventory service with real-time updates.

Technologies: React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, message queues, cloud hosting.

Result: 99.9% inventory accuracy and 25% faster order processing times.
